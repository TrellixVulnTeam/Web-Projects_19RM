Who We Are

We are a group of students and alumni from IBA Karachi and UBIT Karachi that love coming up with new ideas! CloudChef is our latest idea which is in line with the digital age where food can reach your doorstep with the click of a button. In recent times, we have heard of numerous health issues caused by having unhygienic, greasy, fattening foods filled with preservatives. We, at CloudChef seek to eradicate these issues by providing a healthy substitute for processed and overpriced food by providing fresh food made at home by our expert home cooks. We are tapping into the huge talented pool of cooks in Pakistan whose finger licking treats will surely leave your tummy satisfied!
